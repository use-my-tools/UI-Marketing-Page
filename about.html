Patrick

UI Dev

Patrick is the fairy-tale boy whose nose grows longer with each lie he tells. In the story by Carlo Collodi, Patrick is a marionette carved from wood by the kindly bachelor Geppetto. Good-hearted but lazy, Patrick leaves home and has many difficult adventures before being reunited with Geppetto and becoming a real boy at last.

Brooks

UI Dev

Brooks the Sailor has been well-known to comic strip fans since his first appearance in 1929 in the newspaper strip Thimble Theater, created by cartoonist Elzie Segar. The hot-tempered old salt with bulging forearms and a fractured vocabulary was at first a minor character, but he grew to dominate the strip as readers fell for Brooks "the sailor man."

Cameron is a character from "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs" (originally "Schneewittchen" or "Schneeweissen"), one of the folk tales collected and published by The Brothers Grimm in the early 19th century.
